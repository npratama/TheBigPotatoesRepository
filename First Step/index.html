ü•¶Introducing the 2 main cast of the show, Potato And Chips!ü•¶

Potato's Characteristics
  1. Sleeps more than 27 hours a day.
    • Dreams of seeds, popcorn, and broccoli.
  2. Eats more than 10 times her body weight in seeds.
    • Causes her to poop 739 times a day.
  3. Fluffy, fluffy, fluffy, oh so FLUFFY.
    • Suspected to be 20% part bunny, 80% part hamster.
Chip's Characterisctics
  1. Hyperactive and can run on the wheel for 826053769 seconds in one go.
    • Assumes that running on the wheel will bring him miles away from his hooman.
  2. Loves eating roasted worms more than anything else in the world.
    • The main reason why his hamster breath is stinky.
  3. Loves biting his hooman.
    • Payback for dropping him that ONE TIME.

ü•¶And so the story begins...ü•¶

One day, Potato and Chips were lying flat on the wheel after their midnight run, panting. All of a sudden, Chips decided to ask Potato a question, IN ENGLISH.

Here is how their conversation went :~

Chips : Do you like the smell of bread?
Potato : The smell of bed?
Chips : Yes, the smell of bread. You know when you walk into the bread shop, there's such a nice smell?
Potato : Umm... I haven't walked into a bed shop before.
Chips : Then who usually buys your bread for you?
Potato : Nobody. The bed came with this house when this house was bought.
Chips : Umm...huh?
Potato : I guess the last time I've had this bed was since I was 7 hooman years old. (Potato is now 21 hooman years old)
Chips : You've had the same loaf of bread for...14 years?
Potato : Umm...it's still a good bed.
Chips : How are you still alive after eating that?
Potato : Umm...? Eat what? My bed?

ü•¶A moment of silence passes as the 2 stare at each other...and suddenly they both burst out squeak laughing.ü•¶

Potato : YOU MEAN BREAD, NOT BED!
Chips : YES I SAID BREAED RIGHT FROM THE BEGINNING!
Potato : NO WONDER YOU ASKED ME IF I LIKED THE SMELL OF BREAD, I WAS THINKING WHY ARE YOU ASKING ME IF I LIKE THE SMELL OF BEDS!
Chips : YES AND YOU EVEN SAID YOUR BREAD CAME WITH THE HOUSE SINCE YOU WERE 7 HOOMAN YEARS OLD!
Potato : *SQUEAK* *SQUEAK* *SQUEAK* HOW CAN I BE EATING BREAD THAT IS 14 YEARS OLD!
Chips : EXACTLY!

ü•¶And thus, their silly, meaningless conversation came to an end, and they both curled up over their pee spots and fell asleep.ü•¶
